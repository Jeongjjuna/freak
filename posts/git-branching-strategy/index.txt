<h2 id="2-trunk-based-development">2. Trunk Based Development</h2>
<p>모든 개발자가 <code>main</code>에 직접 커밋하거나, 수명이 짧은 브랜치(1~2일)만 사용한다.</p>
<pre><code>main ←── feature/login (1일)
     ←── fix/null-check (반나절)
     ←── feature/payment (2일)
</code></pre>
<p><strong>핵심 원칙</strong>:</p>
<ul>
<li>브랜치 수명은 최대 2일</li>
<li>매일 최소 1번 main에 통합</li>
<li>Feature Flag로 미완성 기능 숨기기</li>
</ul>
<figure data-rehype-pretty-code-figure=""><pre style="background-color:#282A36;color:#F8F8F2" tabindex="0" data-language="java" data-theme="dracula"><code data-language="java" data-theme="dracula" style="display: grid;"><span data-line=""><span style="color:#6272A4">// Feature Flag 예시</span></span>
<span data-line=""><span style="color:#FF79C6">if</span><span style="color:#F8F8F2"> (featureFlags.</span><span style="color:#50FA7B">isEnabled</span><span style="color:#F8F8F2">(</span><span style="color:#E9F284">"</span><span style="color:#F1FA8C">new-payment-ui</span><span style="color:#E9F284">"</span><span style="color:#F8F8F2">, userId)) {</span></span>
<span data-line=""><span style="color:#FF79C6">    return</span><span style="color:#F8F8F2"> newPaymentService.</span><span style="color:#50FA7B">process</span><span style="color:#F8F8F2">(req);</span></span>
<span data-line=""><span style="color:#F8F8F2">} </span><span style="color:#FF79C6">else</span><span style="color:#F8F8F2"> {</span></span>
<span data-line=""><span style="color:#FF79C6">    return</span><span style="color:#F8F8F2"> legacyPaymentService.</span><span style="color:#50FA7B">process</span><span style="color:#F8F8F2">(req);</span></span>
<span data-line=""><span style="color:#F8F8F2">}</span></span></code></pre></figure>
<p><strong>장점</strong>: 지속적 통합. 머지 충돌 최소화.
<strong>단점</strong>: 강력한 CI/CD와 테스트 커버리지가 전제됨.</p>
