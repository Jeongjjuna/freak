<span data-line=""><span style="color:#FF79C6">    private</span><span style="color:#8BE9FD;font-style:italic"> List</span><span style="color:#F8F8F2">&#x3C;Comment> comments;</span></span>
<span data-line=""><span style="color:#F8F8F2">}</span></span></code></pre></figure>
<p><code>EAGER</code>로 바꿔도 해결이 안 된다. JPQL은 fetch 전략을 무시하고 별도 쿼리를 날린다.</p>
<h2 id="3-fetch-join으로-해결">3. Fetch Join으로 해결</h2>
<figure data-rehype-pretty-code-figure=""><pre style="background-color:#282A36;color:#F8F8F2" tabindex="0" data-language="java" data-theme="dracula"><code data-language="java" data-theme="dracula" style="display: grid;"><span data-line=""><span style="color:#F8F8F2">@</span><span style="color:#8BE9FD;font-style:italic">Query</span><span style="color:#F8F8F2">(</span><span style="color:#E9F284">"</span><span style="color:#F1FA8C">SELECT p FROM Post p JOIN FETCH p.comments</span><span style="color:#E9F284">"</span><span style="color:#F8F8F2">)</span></span>
<span data-line=""><span style="color:#8BE9FD;font-style:italic">List</span><span style="color:#FF79C6">&#x3C;</span><span style="color:#F8F8F2">Post</span><span style="color:#FF79C6">></span><span style="color:#50FA7B"> findAllWithComments</span><span style="color:#F8F8F2">();</span></span></code></pre></figure>
<p>쿼리 한 방에 해결된다.</p>
<figure data-rehype-pretty-code-figure=""><pre style="background-color:#282A36;color:#F8F8F2" tabindex="0" data-language="sql" data-theme="dracula"><code data-language="sql" data-theme="dracula" style="display: grid;"><span data-line=""><span style="color:#FF79C6">SELECT</span><span style="color:#F8F8F2"> p.</span><span style="color:#FF79C6">*</span><span style="color:#F8F8F2">, c.</span><span style="color:#FF79C6">*</span></span>
<span data-line=""><span style="color:#FF79C6">FROM</span><span style="color:#F8F8F2"> post p</span></span>
<span data-line=""><span style="color:#FF79C6">INNER JOIN</span><span style="color:#F8F8F2"> comment c </span><span style="color:#FF79C6">ON</span><span style="color:#BD93F9"> c</span><span style="color:#F8F8F2">.</span><span style="color:#BD93F9">post_id</span><span style="color:#FF79C6"> =</span><span style="color:#BD93F9"> p</span><span style="color:#F8F8F2">.</span><span style="color:#BD93F9">id</span></span></code></pre></figure>
<p>단, <strong>컬렉션 Fetch Join은 페이징 불가</strong>하다. <code>HibernateJpaDialect: HHH90003004</code> 경고와 함께 인메모리 페이징이 일어난다.</p>
<h2 id="4-entitygraph">4. @EntityGraph</h2>
<figure data-rehype-pretty-code-figure=""><pre style="background-color:#282A36;color:#F8F8F2" tabindex="0" data-language="java" data-theme="dracula"><code data-language="java" data-theme="dracula" style="display: grid;"><span data-line=""><span style="color:#F8F8F2">@</span><span style="color:#8BE9FD;font-style:italic">EntityGraph</span><span style="color:#F8F8F2">(</span><span style="color:#BD93F9">attributePaths</span><span style="color:#FF79C6"> =</span><span style="color:#F8F8F2"> {</span><span style="color:#E9F284">"</span><span style="color:#F1FA8C">comments</span><span style="color:#E9F284">"</span><span style="color:#F8F8F2">, </span><span style="color:#E9F284">"</span><span style="color:#F1FA8C">tags</span><span style="color:#E9F284">"</span><span style="color:#F8F8F2">})</span></span>
<span data-line=""><span style="color:#8BE9FD;font-style:italic">List</span><span style="color:#FF79C6">&#x3C;</span><span style="color:#F8F8F2">Post</span><span style="color:#FF79C6">></span><span style="color:#50FA7B"> findAll</span><span style="color:#F8F8F2">();</span></span></code></pre></figure>
<p>Fetch Join보다 선언적으로 쓸 수 있다. 하지만 여러 컬렉션을 동시에 사용하면 MultipleBagFetchException이 발생한다.</p>
