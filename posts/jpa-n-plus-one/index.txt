<h2 id="2-왜-발생하나">2. 왜 발생하나</h2>
<p>기본 fetch 전략이 <code>LAZY</code>이기 때문이다. 연관 엔티티는 실제로 접근할 때 쿼리가 나간다.</p>
<figure data-rehype-pretty-code-figure=""><pre tabindex="0" data-language="java" data-theme="github-light dracula"><code data-language="java" data-theme="github-light dracula" style="display: grid;"><span data-line=""><span style="--shiki-light:#24292E;--shiki-dark:#F8F8F2">@</span><span style="--shiki-light:#D73A49;--shiki-light-font-style:inherit;--shiki-dark:#8BE9FD;--shiki-dark-font-style:italic">Entity</span></span>
<span data-line=""><span style="--shiki-light:#D73A49;--shiki-dark:#FF79C6">public</span><span style="--shiki-light:#D73A49;--shiki-dark:#FF79C6"> class</span><span style="--shiki-light:#6F42C1;--shiki-dark:#8BE9FD"> Post</span><span style="--shiki-light:#24292E;--shiki-dark:#F8F8F2"> {</span></span>
<span data-line=""><span style="--shiki-light:#24292E;--shiki-dark:#F8F8F2">    @</span><span style="--shiki-light:#D73A49;--shiki-light-font-style:inherit;--shiki-dark:#8BE9FD;--shiki-dark-font-style:italic">OneToMany</span><span style="--shiki-light:#24292E;--shiki-dark:#F8F8F2">(</span><span style="--shiki-light:#005CC5;--shiki-dark:#BD93F9">mappedBy</span><span style="--shiki-light:#D73A49;--shiki-dark:#FF79C6"> =</span><span style="--shiki-light:#032F62;--shiki-dark:#E9F284"> "</span><span style="--shiki-light:#032F62;--shiki-dark:#F1FA8C">post</span><span style="--shiki-light:#032F62;--shiki-dark:#E9F284">"</span><span style="--shiki-light:#24292E;--shiki-dark:#F8F8F2">, </span><span style="--shiki-light:#005CC5;--shiki-dark:#BD93F9">fetch</span><span style="--shiki-light:#D73A49;--shiki-dark:#FF79C6"> =</span><span style="--shiki-light:#24292E;--shiki-dark:#F8F8F2"> FetchType.LAZY)</span></span>
<span data-line=""><span style="--shiki-light:#D73A49;--shiki-dark:#FF79C6">    private</span><span style="--shiki-light:#24292E;--shiki-light-font-style:inherit;--shiki-dark:#8BE9FD;--shiki-dark-font-style:italic"> List</span><span style="--shiki-light:#24292E;--shiki-dark:#F8F8F2">&#x3C;</span><span style="--shiki-light:#D73A49;--shiki-dark:#F8F8F2">Comment</span><span style="--shiki-light:#24292E;--shiki-dark:#F8F8F2">> comments;</span></span>

<p><code>EAGER</code>로 바꿔도 해결이 안 된다. JPQL은 fetch 전략을 무시하고 별도 쿼리를 날린다.</p>
